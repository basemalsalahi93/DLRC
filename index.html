1. Community Overview

About DLRC

Dubai Land Residential Complex (DLRC) is a key component of the wider Dubailand area, offering highly affordable and spacious residential units. It is a well-planned, value-driven community primarily catering to mid-income families and professionals seeking quality living away from the city's highest-density areas.

Focus: Apartments

Vibe: Affordable, Family-focused, Commuter-friendly
